Legal Violations

Federal Law Violations

  • Disability Discrimination Act 1992: Systematic discrimination in provision of financial and professional services
  • Corporations Act 2001: Interference with proper corporate governance and director duties
  • Competition and Consumer Act 2010: Interference with legitimate business competition
  • Privacy Act 1988: Unauthorized access and control of personal financial information

International Law Breaches

  • UN Convention on Rights of Persons with Disabilities: Articles 5, 12, 19, 27 - Equality, legal capacity, independent living, work rights
  • International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights: Articles 14, 17, 26 - Fair trial, privacy, equal protection
  • Universal Declaration of Human Rights: Articles 17, 23 - Property rights, work rights
  • International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights: Articles 6, 15 - Work rights, cultural participation

Constitutional Violations

  • Section 51(xxxi): Acquisition of property on just terms - property rights violated without compensation
  • Implied Constitutional Rights: Due process and procedural fairness systematically denied
  • Equal Protection: Discriminatory treatment based on disability status
  • Separation of Powers: Administrative overreach beyond proper statutory authority
